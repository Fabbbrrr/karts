How It Works:

  • Driver-Normalized Index: Accounts for driver skill by comparing performance relative to each driver's average
  • Percentile Ranking: Shows how karts rank across all sessions
  • Cross-Kart Drivers: Drivers who use multiple karts provide the most valuable comparison data
  • Confidence Scores: Higher confidence with more laps and drivers
